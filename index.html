About the Drama

Discover the heartbeat of medical school life

In the high-pressure crucible of medical education, where every heartbeat echoes through silent corridors and every decision weighs heavy with consequence, an extraordinary story of human connection unfolds.

Anatomy of Our Hearts dissects the intricate relationships between aspiring doctors who discover that understanding the human heart demands more than textbook knowledge. As they navigate cadavers and cardiograms, they confront the most complex anatomy of all—their own emotions.

This groundbreaking production merges surgical precision with theatrical passion, creating Nigeria's first-ever medical school stage drama that pulses with authenticity and emotional depth.
